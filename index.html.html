About Me

I am a passionate software engineer and 3D artist, with expertise in programming languages such as Python, C, C#, C++, Lua, and GDScript. Over the years, I’ve developed a strong foundation in software development, building scalable applications and intricate systems. My love for game development has led me to work with game engines like Unity, Godot, and Unreal, where I’ve created immersive, interactive experiences. In addition, I combine my technical knowledge with artistic flair to craft detailed 3D models, environments, and assets using tools like Blender and AutoCAD, ensuring that both performance and quality are optimized. I believe in collaboration and open communication, working closely with other engineers, designers, and artists to bring a shared vision to life. My goal is to create experiences that resonate with users by merging creativity, technology, and storytelling. Constantly striving to learn and innovate, I push the boundaries of what’s possible in both the technical and creative domains, whether through building games or developing new software solutions. I am always excited about the next challenge and the opportunity to make a lasting impact with my work.
